个人与实验室简介

我是北京邮电大学副教授、研究员、博士生导师，长期从事光电子集成电路、智能计算、光传感、光互连及片上系统集成方向的研究。以第一作者及共同作者在 Nature Photonics 等发表高水平学术论文 30 余篇，研制世界首台百纳米生物粒子光电智能传感平台，原创性提出百万 TOPS 通用光学计算芯片、架构及系统，申请国家发明专利 10 余项，主持/参与国家自然科学基金重大/重点项目、科技部重点研发青年科学家项目、科技部科技创新2030重大项目及国家重点研发计划项目等多项。

About & Lab

I am an Associate Professor and Researcher at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, supervising PhD students. My research focuses on photonic/electronic integration, intelligent computation, optical sensing, optical interconnects and system-on-chip integration. I have authored 30+ high-impact papers (including Nature Photonics), developed the world's first sub-100nm bio-particle optoelectronic sensing platform, proposed a million-TOPS general-purpose optical computing chip and architecture, and hold 10+ invention patents.
